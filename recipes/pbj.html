Gaby's Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Some peanut butter
  • Some jelly but you best believe it better be grape
  • Optional: butter

Steps

  1. Shmear some peanut butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Shmear some jelly onto the peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  3. Close that mamma jamma bing bong up.
  4. If you ain't a weenie, melt some butter into a pan and toast both sides of the sandwich.
